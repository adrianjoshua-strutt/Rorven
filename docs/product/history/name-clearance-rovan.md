# Preliminary name clearance: Rovan

Status: **Failed for public software branding**  
Checked: 2026-06-16  
Scope: preliminary public-source screening, not legal advice

## Executive conclusion

`Rovan` should not be treated as a cleared product name for a public software project.
It may remain an internal codename temporarily, but public release, package publication,
domain acquisition, branding, or trademark filing should use a different name unless a
qualified trademark professional completes a jurisdiction- and class-specific review.

The decisive conflict is an active company named **Rovan Software Solutions Private
Limited** that has sold education ERP software under the ROVAN name for many years and
states in its own terms that “ROVAN” is its registered trademark. This creates a direct
same-industry collision rather than a merely incidental surname or unrelated product use.

## Decision

1. Keep `Rovan` only as a temporary private codename while a replacement is selected.
2. Do not publish packages, containers, documentation sites, social accounts, or public
   repositories under the Rovan brand.
3. Do not purchase a premium Rovan domain before legal review; domain ownership would
   not remove the software-name conflict.
4. Repeat the same screening process for replacement candidates before adopting one.
5. Reserve the chosen GitHub organization, domains and package scopes immediately after
   a candidate passes preliminary screening.

## Minimum clearance gate for the replacement

A candidate may be adopted publicly only when all of the following are recorded:

- no active same- or adjacent-industry software company using the exact or confusingly
  similar name;
- official searches in EUIPO/TMview, DPMAregister and WIPO Global Brand Database,
  including relevant Nice classes;
- exact GitHub organization or acceptable canonical organization available;
- required domains available or economically obtainable;
- npm, PyPI and container namespace strategy confirmed;
- legal review completed before commercial distribution or trademark filing.
